- Use lower rates (1.2-1.5) if sediment-starved or degraded

## âš ï¸ Common Pitfalls

### 1. Datum Confusion
**Problem**: Mixing tidal datums (Chart Datum, CGVD2013, NAVD88)
**Solution**: Always specify datum, convert consistently

### 2. Temporal Mismatch
**Problem**: Comparing DEMs from different years
**Solution**: Note DEM year, account for SLR (~3.5 mm/year in BC)

### 3. Sediment Supply Assumptions
**Problem**: Assuming adequate sediment without data
**Solution**: Measure accretion directly (sediment pins, Â²Â¹â°Pb dating)

### 4. Linear Extrapolation
**Problem**: Assuming constant carbon accumulation rates
**Solution**: Use exponential/logistic models (Module 11 default)

### 5. Ignoring Lag Times
**Problem**: Expecting immediate carbon benefits
**Solution**: Account for 3-5 year establishment lag in marshes
